Israel, Lebanon agree to extend ceasefire: Trump

US President Donald Trump said on Thursday that Israel and Lebanon agreed to extend a ceasefire that took effect last week. He said it will last for three more weeks.

Trump said: "We had a great meeting with the very high officials of Lebanon and very high officials of Israel. And we think that the president of Lebanon and the prime minister of Israel over the next couple of weeks will be coming here."

The original ceasefire was scheduled to last for 10 days. The US has been mediating negotiations to permanently end the fighting.

Trump was asked about a timeline for resuming talks with Iran. He said, "Don't rush me."
